Privacy Policy

What NAT Clipped Is

NAT Clipped is a locally installed desktop application for Windows. It runs on your own computer and is used to capture, trim, and post gaming clips. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Information We Collect

NAT Clipped collects minimal information to function:

  • OBS Connection Settings — host, port, and password used to connect to your local OBS instance. Stored locally on your machine only.
  • Twitch Username — optionally provided by you for social posting features. Stored locally on your machine only.
  • Voice Command Phrases — phrases you record to trigger clips. Audio samples are processed locally and only the resulting text transcript is stored. Raw audio is never retained.
  • Clip Files — video files captured from OBS are stored locally on your machine in folders you configure. We do not upload or transmit clip content without your explicit action.
  • App Configuration — settings such as layout preferences, hotkeys, and overlay configurations. Stored locally in a JSON file on your machine.

Third-Party Services

NAT Clipped integrates with the following third-party services when you use specific features:

  • Google Speech Recognition — when voice commands are enabled, audio captured by your microphone is sent to Google's speech-to-text API for transcription. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OBS WebSocket — communicates with your locally installed OBS Studio instance. No data leaves your machine through this integration.
  • Twitch API — used to authenticate your Twitch account when you use Twitch-connected features. Subject to Twitch's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok API — used to post clips to TikTok when you use that feature. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage telemetry
  • We do not create user accounts or store credentials on external servers
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal information to third parties
  • We do not access your clip files or configuration without your direct action

Data Storage and Retention

All data NAT Clipped generates is stored locally on your machine. Uninstalling the application removes the application files. Your clip files and configuration are stored in folders you control and are not affected by uninstalling unless you choose to delete them.

Children's Privacy

NAT Clipped is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
