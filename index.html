Everything in one place

OBS Integration

Connects directly to OBS via WebSocket. Trigger clips from inside the app, a hotkey, or your voice — mid-game.

Clip Trimmer

Voice Commands

Train custom phrases that trigger clip saves. Say the word and it clips — no hands required.

🖼️

Overlays & Borders

Add text, image, and animated overlays to clips. Build custom clip borders with a drag-and-drop canvas.

🔊

Sound Overlays

Layer sound effects over your clips. Pick the perfect audio moment to punch up a highlight.

Hotkeys

Fully configurable keyboard shortcuts including numpad support. Clip, save, and post without breaking focus.

Clip Folders

Overlay Presets

Per-Clip Layout

Adjust the clip viewer layout independently per clip. Dial in how each video looks before posting.

⚡ How It Works

Four steps to posted

From your game to your feed in under a minute.

1

Connect OBS

Enter your OBS WebSocket details once. NAT Clipped stays connected in the background.

2

Clip It

Hit a hotkey, say a phrase, or click Save Clip. Your clip is grabbed instantly from OBS.

3

Edit It

Trim the clip, add overlays or sound effects, and pick a layout — all inside the app.

4

Post It

Upload directly to TikTok, Twitch, Instagram, and more with one click. Done.
